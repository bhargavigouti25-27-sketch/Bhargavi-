Our Story

Crafted with
intention,
gifted with love.

I'm Bhargavi — a maker, dreamer, and lifelong lover of handmade things. What started as a personal need for meaningful, beautifully crafted objects grew into a small studio where every product is born from patience and purpose.

I work with natural materials — cotton, jute, clay, and wood — chosen for their texture, durability, and the stories they carry. No mass production. No shortcuts. Just honest craft passed down through generations.
